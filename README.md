# Taiwan Adult Alcohol Metabolism and ALDH2 Study

## Background
ALDH2 polymorphism is common in East Asian populations.
This project simulates alcohol metabolism kinetics differences
between normal and mutant genotypes.

## Research Questions
1. Does alcohol metabolism show zero-order behavior at high concentration?
2. Does ALDH2 mutation reduce metabolic rate?
3. Can high school students correctly identify reaction order?

## Methods
- Simulated dataset (n=100 adults)
- Two genotype groups
- Kinetic modeling
- Educational experiment (10 student groups)

## Results
- High concentration shows near zero-order behavior
- Mutant genotype shows reduced metabolism rate
- 70% student correct classification

## Educational Meaning
Demonstrates enzyme saturation kinetics using real-world genetics context.
